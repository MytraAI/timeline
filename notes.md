Product Name:
TimeLine

Problem Statement:
Our robotics company has a tight deadline for producing the next generation of our robotics system. We have a wide range of engineering disciples that all need access to our development schedule. The Technical Program Managers (TPM's) manage the schedule, updating it everyday as a human in the loop (HITL) from all the information sources and software platforms accross the company.
The schedule is managed in smartsheet, which is an acceptable tools for TPM's, but it hard to read for those not in the Gantt everyday. It is also behind a licence paywall.
The goal of TimeLine is to make the schedule visible to everyone at the company.

The Product:
An agent that pulls data from smartsheet and uploads it to a custom UI with company wide access

Information Flow Process:
Technical Program Managers enter data into smartsheet on daily basis - human filter for timelines planning and updates - will also incorporate project schedules in the future
TimeLine Agent downloads timestamped .xls from smartsheet
TimeLine Agent uploads timestamped .xls to google drive?
TimeLine Agent updates TimeLine with newest .xls available

Product Requirements:
Update must happen on 6 hour interval
Update can happen through a manual push
TimeLine must be viewable by all Mytra employees with google log-in
TimeLine must be secure from outside viewers

Features:
All smartsheet lines must be easily viewable and digestible
All lines should reflect status
Collapse all, expand all feature
Collpase all 1 level, expand all 1 level feature
UI should have a filter for removing lines from the schedule
